Favourite Movies

My top 3 movies of all time by Perry Fontana

1. Deadpool

This is my favourite movie as Ryan Reynolds hilariously brings the comic book character to life, with perfect comedic timing. I would highly reccomend to anyone looking for a laugh or wanting to cheer cheer themselves up, this is the perfect movie.

2. Crows Zero

This is a movie based on the anime of the same name and is set in a school of delinquent and violent students who want to raise to the top of the school by fighting. A new student who is aiming to become the head of his fathers yakuza family joins the school as he is unable to achieve this he has to become the top of the school. It stars Shun Oguri and Takayuki Yamada as the main protagonists and is an action packed thriller.

3. The Nightmare Before Christmas

I am not usually a fan of christmas movies however this dark take on the festive season is highly entertaining and filled with catchy music from Danny Elfman(Creator of The Simpsons theme song). In this movie, you can see classic stop motion animation at work which was revolutionary at the time. I would highly reccomend this for the Christmas season.
